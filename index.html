"Darth Data"

My official title is Director. My unofficial title is "The guy who tells you why your sample has keratin in it."

At the Core, we are obsessed with benchmarking. We recently validated our KingFisher SP3 automated workflow against manual prep. The results?

  • Manual Prep: ~700 phosphosites identified.
  • Our Robots: 9,220 phosphosites in a single run.
